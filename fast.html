About FastAid

FastAid is a structured educational platform created for FAST University students. The goal is to provide well-organized notes, summaries, and course resources that match the university curriculum.

Starting with ICT Lab, FastAid will keep expanding into multiple subjects, offering students a complete and reliable space to study efficiently.
